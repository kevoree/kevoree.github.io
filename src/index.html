Manage distributed systems:
Kevoree Web Editor

The Kevoree Web Editor allows you to visualize and edit Kevoree models. After you've made a modification, select a Group and deploy your model.
Wait for it to propagate in your system, and watch your architecture evolve @runtime

Manipulate models at design and runtime: KevScript

KevScript is a scripting language designed to simplify model creation and modifications

Heterogeneous by design

Kevoree allows you to abstract distributed heterogeneous architectures regardless of their implementation platforms
